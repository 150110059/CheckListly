# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 30, 2026**

## Overview

CheckListly is a simple checklist application. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in connection with your use of the app.

## Data Collection

**This application does not collect or share any personal data.**

CheckListly does not:
- Collect any personal information
- Share any data with third parties
- Use analytics or tracking services
- Require account registration or login
- Access your contacts, location, camera, or microphone

All checklist data you create is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue in the [CheckListly repository](https://github.com/150110059/CheckListly).
